Your Trainer ID numbers

Your Trainer ID (TID) is visible in your Trainer Card in-game. Your Secret ID (SID) is hidden — it's calculated from the frame you land on when you start a new game. Enter both here if you know them, or just start with your TID and guess your target SID from Lincoln's tool.

Your shiny value (TSV)

This is the number your trainer "is." Any Pokémon whose own shiny value matches this number will be shiny for you. You can't change it — it's locked to your TID + SID combo.
